<section id="about" class="card">

<h2>What Does Screen Smart Mean?</h2>

<p>
Being <strong>Screen Smart</strong> means using technology responsibly and understanding how much time we spend on phones, computers, and social media.
</p>

<p>
Technology helps us communicate and learn, but too much screen time may affect concentration, sleep, and relationships.
</p>

</section>

<section id="effects" class="card">

<h2>Effects of Too Much Screen Time</h2>

<h3>Mental Effects</h3>
<ul>
<li>Stress and anxiety</li>
<li>Lower self-confidence</li>
<li>Reduced focus</li>
</ul>

<h3>Physical Effects</h3>
<ul>
<li>Eye strain</li>
<li>Headaches</li>
<li>Poor posture</li>
</ul>

<h3>Social Effects</h3>
<ul>
<li>Less face-to-face interaction</li>
<li>Less time with family and friends</li>
</ul>

</section>

<div class="image-section">

<img src="https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1517245386807-bb43f82c33c4" alt="friends talking offline">

</div>

<blockquote>
Technology should improve our lives, not control them.
</blockquote>

<section id="tips" class="card">

<h2>Tips to Become Screen Smart</h2>

<ul>
<li>Set limits for social media use</li>
<li>Turn off notifications while studying</li>
<li>Take regular breaks from screens</li>
<li>Spend time outdoors</li>
<li>Talk to friends and family in person</li>
</ul>

</section>
